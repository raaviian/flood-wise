What is FloodWise

Recognizing the vital need for a technologically advanced solution, the proposed "FloodWise: Smart Flood Alert and Monitoring System" initiative seeks to solve these pressing concerns. The project intends to revolutionize flood monitoring, prediction, and response by leveraging the potential of Internet of Things (IoT) technology and real- time data processing, and electricity cut off automation. It aims to develop a comprehensive and user-friendly system capable of supplying authorities, emergency responders, and impacted communities with exact, real-time flood-related information. In doing so, the initiative aims to close existing gaps in flood management, ultimately improving disaster preparedness and response skills to better protect lives and infrastructure during floods
